Scrambled Eggs

Description

This is how I make scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • Eggs (4)
  • Milk (1/4 cup)
  • Frank's Buffalo Sauce (1 tablespoon)

Steps

  1. Add eggs, milk, and buffalo sauce into mixing bowl
  2. Put frying pan on stove top and turn on burner
  3. Once it's hot, start cooking!
